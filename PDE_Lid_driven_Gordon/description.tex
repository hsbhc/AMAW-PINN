$$Lid-driven_Gordon \ \ equation$$
$$u \cdot \nabla u + \nabla p -\frac{1}{Re} \Delta u = 0$$
$$x\in [0,1] ,y \in [0,1]$$
$$\nabla \cdot u = 0$$
$$u(x,y) = (1,0) , \ \ y=1$$
$$u(x,y) = (0,0) , \ \ x=0,x=1,y=0$$
$$Re=100$$\\
$$\bold{u(x,y)}=(u(x,y),v(x,y))$$
$$uu_{x}+vu_{y}+p_x -\frac{1}{Re}(u_{xx}+u_{yy}) = 0$$
$$uv_{x}+vv_{y}+p_y -\frac{1}{Re}(v_{xx}+v_{yy}) = 0$$
$$u_{x}+v_{y}= 0$$
